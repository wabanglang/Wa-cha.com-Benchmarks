Any permission granted by project files is limited, conditional, revocable where permitted by law, and subject to the applicable source terms, contributor terms, provenance rules, and release review.

## Stewardship standard

The Project Materials should be developed and distributed with traceability, attribution, provenance, safety review, documentation, and preservation of author identity.

## Derivatives

Derivatives must preserve attribution, applicable notices, provenance records, and review status. Derivatives must not imply sponsorship, approval, ownership transfer, or official project status without written approval from the Project Owner.

Enterprise deployment, resale, hosted service deployment, model wrapping, dataset conversion, private integration, or paid consulting use should be reviewed under the Commercial License Template.
