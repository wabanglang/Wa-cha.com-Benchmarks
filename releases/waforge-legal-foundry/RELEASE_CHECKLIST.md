# Release Checklist

Before publishing a WaForge release, confirm:

- Source terms are included.
- Attribution is included.
- Provenance records are included.
- Checksums are included when artifacts are generated.
- Private materials have been screened.
- Third-party materials are identified.
- Commercial use status is clear.
- Derivative status is clear.
- Official status is clear.
- Known limitations are documented.
- RWAL score is recorded.
- Manifest is present.
- Branch and commit references are recorded.
