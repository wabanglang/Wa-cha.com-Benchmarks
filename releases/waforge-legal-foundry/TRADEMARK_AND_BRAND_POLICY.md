# Trademark and Brand Policy

## Protected names

The following project names, labels, marks, and styled references are reserved for official project use unless approved in writing:

- WaForge
- Wa!
- Wa-cha!
- WaShield
- RWAL
- ABS
- 1CB
- 1dfa1cb
- WAFORGE1

## Permitted reference use

You may refer to the project by name for accurate identification, commentary, review, compatibility statements, and attribution.

## Unofficial use

Forks, wrappers, derivatives, hosted services, packages, and integrations must not imply official status, approval, sponsorship, or ownership by the Project Owner unless approved in writing.

## Naming derivatives

Derivative projects should use a distinct name and should state their relationship to the original project in a factual way.

## Goodwill

All goodwill in official project names and marks belongs to the Project Owner.

## Examples

Allowed: "Compatible with WaForge source materials."

Not allowed without approval: "Official WaForge Cloud" or "WaForge Enterprise" operated by a third party.
