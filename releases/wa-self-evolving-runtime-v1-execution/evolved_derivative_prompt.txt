3. SUBSYSTEM DECISION MODEL
==========================================================================================

- STANDALONE_GAIN

- DETERMINISM_GAIN
- FIDELITY_GAIN
- PORTABILITY_GAIN

COST:
- RISK_COST
- COMPLEXITY_COST
- REDUNDANCY_COST

NET_SCORE:
average(GAIN) * 10 - average(COST) * 5

Decision thresholds:

- append_replace: NET_SCORE >= 85 and no fatal S4 risk
- addin_merge:    70 <= NET_SCORE < 85
- hold:           50 <= NET_SCORE < 70
- discard:        NET_SCORE < 50 or any fatal S4 risk
